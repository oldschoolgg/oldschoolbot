---
title: "Drift Net Fishing"
---

Drift net fishing is a hybrid [[fishing]] + [[hunter]] activity.

Start with:
[[/activities driftnet_fishing minutes\:X]]

Optional:
- `no_stams:true` to skip stamina use (and lose that boost)

## Requirements

- [[hunter:44]]
- [[fishing:47]]
- Graceful top, legs, and gloves equipped
- Merfolk trident or Uncharged trident equipped
- Drift nets in bank (you consume one per catch cycle)

## Boosts

- Flippers equipped: +50% speed
- No Flippers: -50% speed
- Stamina potion(4): +30% speed (consumes 1 per trip)
- Ring of endurance (charged or uncharged): +6% speed when using stamina

## XP Expectations

Rates depend heavily on boosts and trip length. With strong setup (Flippers + stamina + ring), typical outcomes are roughly:

- Fishing: up to around 90k/hr at high levels
- Hunter: up to around 120k/hr at high levels

Without boosts, rates are substantially lower.

## Rewards

Base loot includes drift-net table rewards (for example oyster, numulite, pufferfish, fossils, medium clues), plus bonus fish based on your Fishing level:

- [[fishing:50]]: raw lobster
- [[fishing:60]]: raw swordfish
- [[fishing:70]]: raw shark
- [[fishing:80]]: raw sea turtle
- [[fishing:90]]: raw manta ray

Notes:

- This activity does not roll the Heron pet.
- Bring enough drift nets for your chosen trip length.
