Starting at 35 Fishing, you can fight Tempoross with:
[[/k name\:Tempoross]]

## Speed Boosts

Base duration is 15 minutes per kill, then reduced by:

- Fishing level boost: up to 10% total
- KC learning boost: up to 10% total at 100 KC
- Harpoon boost (in skilling setup):
  - Dragon harpoon (61+): 10%
  - Infernal harpoon (75+): 10%
  - Crystal harpoon (71+): 30%

## Reward Boosts

- Infernal harpoon gives 100% more reward rolls.
- Dragon/Crystal harpoon do not increase reward roll count.

## XP Formula

Fishing XP per kill is based on level:

- `5500 * (Fishing level / 40)`
- Then Angler outfit XP bonus is applied (up to +2.5% in skilling setup)

## Approximate XP/hr (High-KC Setup)

Approximate values with 100 KC learning bonus and full Angler:

| Level | Crystal Harpoon | Dragon/Infernal Harpoon | No Harpoon |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 71 | ~70k/hr | ~55k/hr | ~48k/hr |
| 80 | ~79k/hr | ~62k/hr | ~54k/hr |
| 90 | ~89k/hr | ~70k/hr | ~61k/hr |
| 99 | ~98k/hr | ~77k/hr | ~68k/hr |

## Rewards

- Rewards are rolled automatically when your trip ends.
- Unique replacement behavior:
  - If you already own Fish barrel, Tackle box, Big harpoonfish, or Tiny tempor and roll it again, you receive 25 soaked pages instead.
- Spirit flakes can be used in normal fishing for extra fish rolls.
