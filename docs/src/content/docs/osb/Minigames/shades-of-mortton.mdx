The Shades of Mort'ton minigame works in a condensed manner compared to OSRS. Your minion only needs logs and shade remains to send trips—no need to collect sacred oil or burn the shades yourself, as it's all done in one trip. There are no skill requirements to start Shades of Mort'ton.

To start a trip of the minigame, use the following:

- [[/minigames shades_of_morton start shade\:shadeType logs\:logType]]

## Boosts

- 50% more Prayer XP for completion of the Morytania hard diary.  
- 50% more Firemaking XP for completion of the Morytania elite diary.

## Creating Coffins

To create the Bronze coffin, first purchase the Broken coffin:

- [[/buy name\:Broken coffin]]

After that, each new tier is unlocked by obtaining the next set of locks. Upgrades are sequential and cannot be skipped. Each tier gives an additional minute to Shades of Mort'ton trip time when equipped in any setup.

- Bronze coffin: No boost  
- Steel coffin: +1 minute  
- Black coffin: +2 minutes  
- Silver coffin: +3 minutes  
- Gold coffin: +4 minutes
