Dark fishing bait and raw karambwanji are not sold by the bot. You must obtain them yourself or buy from other players.

## Fishing System

Fishing now uses tick-based simulation (0.6s ticks) with per-catch rolls.

- Multi-fish spots (for example Trout/Salmon) track each subfish separately.
- Rada's blessing and Spirit flakes can add extra fish per catch roll.
- Extra fish from blessing/flakes do not give extra XP and do not add extra clue/pet rolls.
- Powerfishing uses faster timing (where supported), drops fish, and disables Spirit flakes.
- Barbarian cut-eat is supported while powerfishing Barbarian fishing at 99 Fishing and 80 Cooking.
- Moon key halves roll per catch on eligible fish after Children of the Sun.

## Boosts

### Angler Outfit

Obtainable from Fishing Trawler and [Aerial Fishing](/osb/activities/aerial-fishing/). When equipped in skilling setup:

- Angler hat: +0.4% Fishing XP
- Angler top: +0.8% Fishing XP
- Angler waders: +0.6% Fishing XP
- Angler boots: +0.2% Fishing XP
- Full outfit: +2.5% Fishing XP total

### Spirit Flakes

- 50% chance at extra fish per catch roll
- Extra fish gives no additional XP
- Disabled while powerfishing

### Rada's Blessing

When equipped (and not powerfishing), grants extra fish chance per catch:

- Rada's blessing 1: 2%
- Rada's blessing 2: 4%
- Rada's blessing 3: 6%
- Rada's blessing 4: 8%

### Tools

The following tools give a generic 5% speed boost in their method:

- Pearl fishing rod (bait fishing)
- Pearl fly fishing rod (fly fishing)
- Pearl barbarian rod (barbarian fishing)
- Crystal harpoon (harpooning/caging/netting)

Extra harpoon boosts for Tuna/Swordfish and Shark spots:

- Crystal harpoon: +35%
- Dragon harpoon: +20%
- Infernal harpoon: +20%

### Storage

- Fish barrel or fish sack barrel adds 28 inventory slots and extends trips by 9 minutes (non-minnow, non-powerfishing).

### Moon Key

After Children of the Sun, eligible fish have per-catch moon key half rolls:

- Trout/Salmon: 1/4100
- Tuna/Swordfish: 3/5000
- Lobster: 3/5000
- Shark: 1/580

## Notable Requirements

- Karambwanji (bait for Karambwan): 15 QP
- Monkfish: 100 QP
- Anglerfish: 40 QP
- Minnows: full Angler outfit owned

## Training

### Fastest XP Route To 99 (Active)

This route is based on current `/fish` simulation behavior and assumes you are powerfishing once it becomes efficient.

- [[/fish name\:Shrimp/Anchovies]] from [[fishing:1]] to [[fishing:5]]
- [[/fish name\:Sardine/Herring]] from [[fishing:5]] to [[fishing:20]] (requires fishing bait)
- [[/fish name\:Trout/Salmon powerfish\:true]] from [[fishing:20]] to [[fishing:50]] (requires feathers)
- [[/fish name\:Tuna/Swordfish powerfish\:true]] from [[fishing:50]] to [[fishing:58]]
- [[/fish name\:Barbarian fishing powerfish\:true]] from [[fishing:58]] to [[fishing:99]] (requires feathers, plus [[agility:15]] and [[strength:15]])

Notes:

- At [[agility:30]] and [[strength:30]] you start catching leaping salmon while barbarian fishing.
- At [[agility:45]] and [[strength:45]] you start catching leaping sturgeon.
- For lower-effort training with good rewards, [Tempoross](/osb/activities/tempoross/) is still excellent, but it is slower XP than high-efficiency powerfishing at high levels.

### Best Pet Chance

Currently, Karambwans are one of the fastest practical Heron methods.

### Additional Information
