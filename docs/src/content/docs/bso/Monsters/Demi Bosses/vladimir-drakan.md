## Vladimir Drakan

Kill with [[/k name\:Vladimir Drakan]] (alias: `vd`). Uses **Ranged** setup.

## Requirements

- [[qp:160]]
- [[hitpoints:120]]
- Per kill cost: 1 [[Vial of blood]] + 1 [[Silver stake]]
- Requires equipped ammo: [[Silver bolts]]

## Core mechanics

- Kill time: 15 minutes each.
- Supplies needed are food-based (high heal requirement: 500 total heal per kill).
- Axe boost: [[Axe of the high sungod]] gives **+10%** speed (works from bank).
- POH boost: [[Ancient rejuvenation pool]] gives **+5%** speed.

## Drops

Always/regular table includes:

- [[Vampyre dust]] (always)
- Blood orange seeds (5-10)
- Coins (100k-300k)
- Dragon bones (30-50)
- Crystal keys (3-5)
- Rune/adamant gear and jewellery bundles

Tertiaries:

- [[Blightbrand]] — **1/512**
- [[Clue scroll (elite)]] — **1/50**
- [[Clue scroll (master)]] — **1/100**
- [[Clue scroll (grandmaster)]] — **1/256**

Special rolls per kill:

- [[Echo]]: starts at **1/1000**, gets rarer with CL scaling (**x1.5 per Echo in CL**)
- [[Vampyric plushie]]: starts at **1/250**, gets rarer with CL scaling (**x2 per plushie in CL**)
- Vampyre hunter outfit piece: if missing any piece, **1/32** to roll one random missing piece
