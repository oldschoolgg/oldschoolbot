## Solis
 
Solis is a high-level melee only boss. Igne tames cannot be used to kill Solis. 
It is most efficient to kill one instance of the boss per trip to receive double loot on every kill.
 
Solis drops **Solite** and materials used to craft the **Axe of the High Sungod**.

## Requirements
 
- [[attack:110]] [[strength:110]] [[defence:110]] [[magic:110]] [[ranged:110]] [[hitpoints:120]] [[slayer:110]]
- [[qp:2500]]
- Combatant's cape equipped
- Maxed Igne Tame (best gear, all fed items)
- Gorajan warrior set, Drygore rapier, Offhand dragon claw
- Heat res. brew (3x) and Heat res. restore per kill
