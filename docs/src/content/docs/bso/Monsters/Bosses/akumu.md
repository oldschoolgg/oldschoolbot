## Akumu

Akumu is a high-level melee demi-boss. It is unlocked by obtaining at least 1 kill of [Solis](../bosses/solis.md). Igne tames cannot be used to kill Akumu

## Get Started

- [[/k name\:Akumu]]

**Requirements:** [[attack:120]] [[strength:120]] [[defence:120]] [[hitpoints:120]] [[slayer:120]]
**Other Requirements:** Combatant's cape equipped, 1 KC of [Solis](../bosses/solis.md)
**Item cost per kill:** Super combat potion(4), 1x Lumina, 3x Saradomin brew(4), 1x Super restore(4)

## Boosts

- Axe of the high sungod – 20%
- Ancient Rejuvenation Pool – 5%

## Rewards

Akumu guarantees 5–10 Nightmarish ashes per kill, alongside mystery boxes, battlestaves, black dragonhide, cannonballs, and uncut gems.
