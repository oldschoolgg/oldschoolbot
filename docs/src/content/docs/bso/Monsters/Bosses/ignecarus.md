## Ignecarus

Ignecarus is a giant dragon boss. Fire resistance is the most important aspect of the fight, as regular potions are destroyed by the heat — [Heat Resistant Potions](../../skills/herblore) must be used instead.

## Get Started

- [[/k name\:Ignecarus (Solo)]]
- [[/k name\:Ignecarus (Mass)]]

**Requirements:** [[slayer:110]], Heat res. brew and Heat res. restore per kill

## Boosts

- Drygore Longsword – 15%
- Ignis ring (i) – 10%
- TzKal Cape – 6%
- Brawler's Hook Necklace – 4%

## Gear

You have a base death chance of 95%. This is reduced to 47.5% with full [Dwarven armour](/bso/custom-items/#dwarven-equipment) and further reduced to 14.5% with a Dragonfire shield.

Torva can be used as a substitute for Dwarven armour, but death chance will be higher without the fire protection.

## Rewards

Ignecarus drops large quantities of metal bars, uncut gems, and rune items.

- **[Ignis ring]** – Can be imbued with a Magus scroll from [Vasa Magus](vasa-magus) requiring [[crafting:120]] [[magic:105]], to create the Ignis ring (i). Requires [[defence:105]] to equip.
- **Ignecarus scales** – Used with Abyssal dragon bones to create Dragon's fury potion (2 scales + 1 Abyssal dragon bones per potion). Can also be used to create Hellfire Arrows (1 scale + 1 headless arrow → 5 arrows).
- **Ignecarus dragonclaw** – Used with Dragon's fury, Twisted bow, Zaryte bow, and 3 Smouldering stones to create a [Hellfire Bow](/bso/custom-items/#hellfire-bow-&-arrows).
- **Dragon egg** – Can be placed in a nursery to hatch into a [dragon tame](../../tames/igne-tame). Eggs are tradeable, but cannot be retrieved or traded once placed in a nursery.
