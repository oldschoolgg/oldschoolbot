## Venatrix

Venatrix is a spider boss fought with your melee setup. Igne tames cannot be used to kill Venatrix.

## Get Started

- [[/k name\:Venatrix]]

**Requirements:** [[qp:500]] [[attack:120]] [[strength:120]] [[defence:120]] [[hitpoints:120]] [[slayer:120]]

To unlock Venatrix, harvest a **Toxic zygomite**. Create Toxic zygomite spores by using a [[Deathly toxic potion]] on any zygomite spore, then plant and harvest it.

You must also have 1 KC of [Celestara](../bosses/celestara.md) before fighting Venatrix.

**Min. Gear:** Melee weapon with at least 134 slash attack (equivalent to a Soulreaper axe)
**Item cost per kill:** 2x Anti-venom+(4), 1x Saradomin brew(4)

## Boosts

- Axe of the high sungod – 20%
- Ancient Rejuvenation Pool – 5%

## Rewards

- **Venatrix eggs** – Open for a 1/1000 chance at the Baby venatrix pet
- **Spiders leg bottom** – Can be combined with the Offhand drygore rapier to create the [Offhand spidergore rapier](/bso/custom-items/#offhand-spidergore-rapier), which provides speed boosts at many pieces of content
