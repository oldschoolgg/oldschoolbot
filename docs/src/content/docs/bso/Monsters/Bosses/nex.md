## Nex

Nex is an endgame ranged boss. It can be fought solo or as a mass. Unique drops include Torva, Pernix, and Virtus gear, as well as TMBs and Grandmaster clues.

## Get Started

- [[/k name\:Nex (Solo)]]
- [[/k name\:Nex (Mass)]]

**Requirements:** [[prayer:95]], Frozen Key

## Boosts

All boosts are divided by team size.

- Full Pernix – 20% (2.4% per piece)
- Zaryte Bow – 20%
- Full Gorajan Archer – 15%

## Gear

Aim for 205+ ranged attack and as high ranged defence as possible to reduce brew/restore usage. A Barbarian Assault arrow in your ammo slot helps with ranged attack bonus, allowing for tankier gear. A speed penalty applies if ranged attack is under 200.

The Abyssal Cape is highly recommended, as it significantly reduces your chance of death.

### Max Ranged Setup

- Full Gorajan Archer
- Farsight Snapshot Necklace
- Venator Ring
- Hellfire Bow / Zaryte Bow
- Abyssal Cape / Combatant's Cape
- Arrow slot makes no difference in max ranged setup

Each kill takes ~8 minutes.

**Warning:** Ironmen will likely die repeatedly with minimum gear. It is recommended to gain KC in masses first.

## Rewards

Nex drops broken armour pieces that must be repaired using components from God Wars sets. Torva uses Bandos pieces, Pernix uses Armadyl, and Virtus uses Ancestral - repaired via [[/create item\:revert]] then [[/create item\:[piece name]]].

- **Torva, Pernix, Virtus** – Can be upgraded to best-in-slot [Gorajan armour](/bso/skills/dungeoneering/#gorajan-armour). Drop rate for hitting the gear table scales with team size; any specific piece is ~1/1600
- **Zaryte Bow** – Boosts Nex performance; used to create the Hellfire Bow
- **Virtus Crystal** – Combine with Mage's Book or Kodai Wand to create Virtus Book or Virtus Wand
- **Ancient hilt** – Used to create the Ancient Godsword
- **Bloodsoaked feather** – Used to create the Nex pet, Nexterminator
- **Ancient emblem** – No current use
