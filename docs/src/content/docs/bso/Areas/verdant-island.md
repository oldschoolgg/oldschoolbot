The Verdant Island is a major content update introducing a new zone with bosses, minigames, skilling activities, equipment, and a deep island progression system that ranges from mid to endgame.

## Island Upgrades

Players collectively invest resources into ten upgrade categories, each with five tiers. All upgrades require **weekly maintenance** to remain active. Failing to maintain these costs suspends bonuses until serviced.

Players can **assign** the island to one category per week. The assigned category gains **+30%** to its effects; all others receive **-20%**.

## Upgrade Categories

| Category                  | Bonus                                               |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Warcamp Fortifications    | Up to 25% faster boss kills (5% per tier)           |
| Archon Sanctum            | Unlocks the Archon; up to +50% Archon loot at T5    |
| Settlement Infrastructure | Up to 25% better minigame rewards (5% per tier)     |
| Expedition Outfitters     | Up to 25% faster gathering (5% per tier)            |
| Astral Observatory        | Up to +2.5% global XP (0.5% per tier)              |
| Fishing Docks             | Passively accumulates fish up to level 120 at T5    |
| Excavation Tunnels        | Passively accumulates ore up to level 120 at T5     |
| Lumberyard                | Passively accumulates logs up to level 120 at T5    |
| Divination Spire          | Passively accumulates energy up to level 120 at T5  |
| Fertile Fields            | Passively accumulates crops up to level 120 at T5   |

The five gathering categories accumulate resources passively, capping at **24 hours**. Content level scales with tier: T1 unlocks up to level 40, T2 up to 60, T3 up to 75, T4 up to 90, T5 up to 120.

## PvM

### Crystalline Sentinel

The most accessible Verdant Island boss, fought with melee and ranged.

**Requirements:** [[attack:70]] [[strength:70]] [[defence:70]] [[hitpoints:70]]
**Min. Gear:** Rune armour, Dragon scimitar, Dragon defender
**QP Required:** 500 | **Kill Time:** ~7 minutes
**Item cost per kill:** Super combat potion(4), 2x Prayer potion(4)

**Boosts:**
- Vitrolic curse (ranged): +25% speed
- Elderflame bow + Elderflame arrows: +10% speed
- Brawler's hook necklace (melee): +10% speed
- Starfire bow + Elderflame arrows: +7% speed

**Notable drops:** Sentinel core (1/1000), Starfire bow (u) (1/3000), Celestyte, Oneiryte, Starfire agate, Crystalline ore, Dense crystal shard, Crystalline plank, Gem infused ore, Elderflame arrowtips, clue scrolls (medium/hard).

### Fungal Behemoth

A dummy boss with a large hp pool. The combat XP multiplier scales inversely with your average combat level. The lower your level (75–90 range), the higher the multiplier.

**Requirements:** [[attack:35]] [[strength:35]] [[defence:35]] [[hitpoints:35]]
**Min. Gear:** Rune armour, Dragon scimitar, Dragon defender
**QP Required:** 750 | **Kill Time:** ~9 minutes 
**Item cost per kill:** Super combat potion(4), 3x Prayer potion(4), 2x Saradomin brew(4)

**Boosts:**
- Vitrolic curse (ranged): +25% speed
- Elderflame bow + Elderflame arrows: +10% speed
- Brawler's hook necklace (melee): +10% speed
- Starfire bow + Elderflame arrows: +7% speed

**Notable drops:** Verdant heart (1/3000), Prolific twine (1/5000), Island Table and Gem Table drops, grimy herbs, seeds, Verdantyte, Starfire agate, Oneiryte, Elderflame arrowtips.

### Orym

**Requirements:** [[attack:115]] [[strength:115]] [[defence:115]] [[magic:115]] [[ranged:115]] [[hitpoints:115]] [[slayer:115]]
**Min. Gear:** Gorajan warrior set, Dragon hunter lance, Avernic defender
**QP Required:** 2,500 | **Kill Time:** ~40 minutes
**Other Requirements:** Combatant's cape equipped; maxed Igne Tame (best gear, all fed items)
**Item cost per kill:** Super combat potion(4), 3x Heat res. brew, Heat res. restore, Enhanced stamina potion

**Boosts:**
- Dragonbane glaive: +25% speed
- Dragonbane aegis: +15% speed
- Searcrown band: +7% speed

**Notable drops:** Primordial Bones (2–5, guaranteed), Primordial Scales (12–50, guaranteed), Forsaken tear, Sacrilegious flask, Primordial heartstring, Primordial spine, Clue scroll (grandmaster) (1/50), Orylin pet (1/5000), Island Table and Island Gem Table rolls.

### Burning Dominion

A group mass encounter ([[/mass]]) where players fight Orym and Orrodil simultaneously. The hardest content in the expansion, with combined drop tables at improved rates.

> **Warning:** Using Saradomin brews instead of Heat res. brews results in instant death for the entire team.

**Boosts:**

### The Archon

A solo boss with a 1-in-50 chance to spawn at the end of any eligible trip (all Verdant Island monsters, Brimstone Distillery, Construction Contracts, Ancient Mycology, Archaic Mining, Gemstone Fishing). A button prompt appears to begin the encounter.

**Requires Archon Sanctum I to be completed before the Archon can be fought.**

Comes in three tiers based on combat XP milestones:

| Tier | Name              | Unlock Condition                    | Base Duration |
| ---- | ----------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------- |
| 1    | Shrouded Archon   | 120 in any combat skill (104.3M XP) | ~5 minutes    |
| 2    | Ascendant Archon  | 1B XP in 2+ combat skills           | ~10 minutes   |
| 3    | Apotheotic Archon | 5B XP in 3+ combat skills           | ~20 minutes   |

Trip duration is reduced by gear score and Warcamp Fortifications bonuses. **Elderflame arrows** are required at a quantity per fight of: 50 / 100 / 200 by tier. Without a Tidal collector (i) or range cape, arrow consumption is **3x higher**.

Gear score is calculated across all three combat setups: Ranged (50% weight), Melee (25%), Mage (25%):

| Setup  | Key Items                                                                                                                    |
| ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Ranged | Elderflame bow, Elderflame arrows, Tidal collector (i), Farsight snapshot necklace, Ring of piercing (i), Gorajan archer set |
| Melee  | Axe of the high sungod, Empyrean armour set, Brawler's hook necklace, Searcrown band, TzKal cape, Vitrolic curse             |
| Mage   | Void staff, Abyssal tome, Arcane blast necklace, Spellbound ring (i), Vasa cloak, Gorajan occult set                        |

## Minigames

### Brimstone Distillery

A Herblore minigame where players distill potions using coal, vials, and ingredients. Each distillation produces 0–4 potions depending on a quality roll. Diluted brimstone is produced as a secondary resource required for further crafting.

**Requirement:** [[herblore:110]] (recipes up to 120)

**Boosts:**
- Celestial pendant: 10% faster distillation
- Enhanced stamina potion: 10% faster distillation (consumed per trip)
- Dragon's fury: 10% faster distillation (consumed per trip)
- Herblore master cape: 10% less coal & catalyst usage
- Full Graceful: improves bad quality rolls (minimum floor of 20)
- Settlement Infrastructure upgrade: % better rewards

**Pet:** Sedryn, rare chance per trip

Primordial Bones are the highest-tier bones on the Verdant Island and can be offered for Prayer XP. Obtained from Orym, Orrodil, the Burning Dominion, and Dragonbone Mining.

## Items & Equipment

### Empyrean Armour

A strict upgrade to Gorajan warrior armour consisting of five pieces: Greathelm, Greatplate, Greatgreaves, Greatgauntlets, and Greatsabaton. All three Gorajan warrior variants (standard, primal, oceanic) can be used as the base, and the output is identical regardless of variant. Empyrean shards are obtained primarily from the Archon.

**Requirements:** [[smithing:120]] [[crafting:120]]

- 1x Gorajan warrior set (any variant)
- 20x Dwarven bar
- 10x Gorajan shards
- 5x Elderflame catalyst
- 2x Sun-metal bar
- 750x Empyrean shards
- 5,000x Dark animica

### Dragonbane Glaive

A high-tier melee weapon providing +25% speed when fighting Orym, Orrodil, or the Burning Dominion.

**Requirements:** [[smithing:120]] [[crafting:120]]

- 7,500x Dark animica
- 3x Dwarven bar
- 1x Primordial spine
- 1x Primordial heartstring
- 1x Dragon hunter lance
- 2,500x Primordial scales

### Dragonbane Aegis

A high-tier shield providing +15% speed when fighting Orym, Orrodil, or the Burning Dominion.

**Requirements:** [[smithing:120]] [[crafting:120]]

- 7,500x Dark animica
- 3x Dwarven bar
- 1x Dragonfire shield
- 1x Dragonfire ward
- 1x Wyvern shield
- 1x Primordial heartstring
- 2,500x Primordial scales

### Celestial Pendant

Provides 10% faster distillation at the Brimstone Distillery and 25% faster construction at Construction Contracts when equipped or in bank.

**Requirements:** [[smithing:120]] [[crafting:120]]

- 7,500x Dark animica
- 1x Dwarven bar
- 1x Primordial spine
- 1x Primordial heartstring
- 1x Shattered pendant

### Prismare Ring

An upgrade to the First Age Ring providing an increased passive XP bonus. Created by combining a Prismare ring (u) (a unique drop from the Archon) with the First Age Ring. Can be reverted (returns Prismare ring (u)) or dismantled (returns 250x Prismare + 250x Empyrean shards).

- 1x Prismare ring (u)
- 1x First age ring
- 1x Elderflame catalyst
- 50x Empyrean shards

### Vitrolic Curse

Provides +25% speed to the Crystalline Sentinel and Fungal Behemoth when equipped in the ranged gear setup. Sacrilegious flask drops from Orym and the Burning Dominion.

**Requirements:** [[prayer:120]]

- 1x Dwarven blessing
- 1x Infernal core
- 1x Sacrilegious flask

### Searcrown Band

A ring providing +7% speed when fighting Orym, Orrodil, or the Burning Dominion. Forsaken tear drops from Orym and the Burning Dominion.

- 1x Ignis ring (i)
- 1x Forsaken tear
- 1x Dwarven bar

### Starfire Bow

Provides +7% speed (with Elderflame arrows) to the Crystalline Sentinel and Fungal Behemoth. Required to craft the Elderflame Bow.

**Requirements:** [[fletching:95]]

- 1x Starfire bow (u): 1/3000 from Crystalline Sentinel
- 1x Prolific twine: 1/5000 from Fungal Behemoth

### Elderflame Bow

An upgrade to the Starfire Bow providing +10% speed (with Elderflame arrows) to the Crystalline Sentinel and Fungal Behemoth. Required ammo bow for Archon encounters.

**Requirements:** [[fletching:110]]

- 1x Starfire bow
- 1x Elderflame catalyst

### Elder Sigil

Assembled from three fragments obtained from Orrodil, the Burning Dominion, and high-tier Construction Contracts.

- 1x Elder sigil fragment (1)
- 1x Elder sigil fragment (2)
- 1x Elder sigil fragment (3)
