title: "Implings"
---

On top of regular implings, BSO adds 5 custom implings:

## Infernal impling (jar)

- Requirement: [[hunter:94]]
- Key drops/rates:
  - [[Clue scroll (hard)]]: 1/10
  - [[Clue scroll (elite)]]: 1/50
  - [[Uncut onyx]]: 1/1,000,000

## Eternal impling (jar)

- Requirement: [[hunter:99]]
- Also requires owning/equipping [[Vasa cloak]] to catch (invisibility mechanic).
- Key drops/rates:
  - [[Clue scroll (grandmaster)]]: 1/8
  - [[Elder scroll piece]]: 1/100
  - [[Eternal crystal]]: 1/1,000,000

## Mystery impling (jar)

- Requirement: [[hunter:105]]
- Rolls include [[Tradeable Mystery Box]], [[Untradeable Mystery Box]], [[Pet Mystery Box]], [[Equippable mystery box]], and clues.
- Key drops/rates:
  - [[Elder scroll piece]]: 1/100
  - Extra [[Mystery impling jar]]: 1/1,000,000

## Chimpling (jar)

- Requirement: [[hunter:95]] and a [[Magic banana]] per catch.
- Key drops/rates:
  - [[Gorilla rumble greegree]]: 1/300
  - [[Monkey egg]]: 1/7000
  - [[Monkey dye]]: 1/3000
  - [[Banana enchantment scroll]]: 1/100
  - [[Elder scroll piece]]: 1/100

## Shrimpling

- Requirement: [[hunter:97]] (underwater impling).
- Key drops/rates:
  - [[Clue scroll (grandmaster)]]: 1/40
  - [[Elder scroll piece]]: 1/80
  - [[Shrimps]] (pet): 1/1,000,000

You can view impling CL progress with [[/cl name\:Implings]].
