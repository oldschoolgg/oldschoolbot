## Custom Content and Boosts

- [Lil' Lamb](/bso/custom-items/pets#miscellaneous-pets) - 2x-4x xp boost from [[/offer]]
- Abyssal Dragon Bones - Bis bones obtained from [Malygos](/bso/monsters/demi-bosses/malygos)
- Royal Dragon Bones - Bis bones obtained from [QBD](/bso/monsters/demi-bosses/queen-black-dragon)
- Gorajan Bonecrusher - Passively crushes bones obtained from [[/k]] trips. Create by combining the [Gorajan Bonecrusher (u)](dungeoneering-training/#starting-out) with 3 gorajan shards
- [Prayer Master Cape](/bso/custom-items/capes/#master-capes) - 40% Less Prayer Potions consumed while using Dwarven Blessing
- [Superior Bonecrusher](/bso/skills/invention/#inventions) - 25% xp boost to the Gorajan Bonecrusher
