### Custom Content and Boosts

- [Harry](/bso/custom-items/pets#miscellaneous-pets) - Increases mark of grace rate and eliminates level-scaling for marks of grace
- [Cob](/bso/custom-items/pets#discontinued-pets) - 2x speed boost at Hallowed Sepulchre
- [Daemonheim agility pass](dungeoneering-training/dg-rewards#miscellaneous-buyables) - Allows access to the best agility course at lvl 101 agility.
- Daemonheim agility course - Chance to find dungeoneering dyes and gorajan shards ([2.5x rate of the dungeoneering skill](dungeoneering-training/dg-rewards#gorajan-shards); i.e. 1/2000 becomes 1/5000)
- Sepulchre Floor 6 and 7 - Unlocked at levels 105 and 115 respectively.
- [[Marimbo's Monkey Rumble]] - While doing this minigame you can receive agility experience at about 1/4th of the strength xp you receive
- [Agility Master Cape](/bso/custom-items/capes/#master-capes) - Acts as a full set of graceful
- [Silverhawk Boots](/bso/skills/invention/#inventions) - 36k/hr passive xp and 1.9x speed boost to [[/laps]]
- [Graceful portent](/bso/skills/divination/#portents) - converts marks of grace found on trips into bonus xp

### Optimal Gear Setup

Cob, Agility master cape, silverhawk boots
