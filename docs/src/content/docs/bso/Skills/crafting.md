## Custom Content and Boosts

- [**Dwarven Greathammer**](/bso/custom-items/#dwarven-equipment) – 2× speed boost to Crafting
- [**Klik**](/bso/custom-items/pets#miscellaneous-pets) – 3× boost when tanning
  _Do not use in conjunction with the Dwarven Greathammer; it overrides the pet perk._
- **Royal dragonhide** – Highest tier hide, obtained from killing the [[Queen Black Dragon]]
- **Royal dragon leather** – Made by tanning Royal dragonhide
- [**Scroll of Dexterity**](dungeoneering-training/dg-rewards#buyable-boosts-utility) – 15% extra crafting output
- **Heat Res. Vial** – Made with molten glass and athelas paste
- [**Master Hammer and Chisel**](/bso/skills/invention/#inventions) – 45% speed boost to Crafting (_does not apply to tanning_)
- [**Portable Tanner**](/bso/skills/invention/#inventions) – Automatically tans hides obtained from [[/k]]
- **Carapace Armour** – Adamant-equivalent gear: helm, legs, gloves, torso, boots

## Optimal Gear Setup

- Use **Klik** when tanning
- Use **Dwarven Greathammer** when crafting
- Equip **Crafting master cape**
- Equip **Materials** for the Master Hammer and Chisel invention
