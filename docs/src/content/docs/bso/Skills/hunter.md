### Custom Content and Boosts

- Elder Birdhouses - can be set at level 99 Hunter, giving random seeds and a chance at [Grandmaster Clues](/bso/clue-scrolls/grandmaster-clues)
- [Scroll of the Hunt](dungeoneering-training/dg-rewards#buyable-boosts-utility) - +4 Birdhouses
- Sand Geckos - can be hunted at 120 Hunter, 99 Agility, and 99 Fishing, consuming 1 simple kibble per 8 geckos. They reward various mystery boxes, and have a chance to give the [Sandy](/bso/custom-items/pets#resource-gathering-and-loot-affecting-pets) pet
- Eastern Ferret - can be hunted at 96 Hunter, consuming 1 delicious kibble per 2 ferrets. Chance at [Zippy](/bso/custom-items/pets#miscellaneous-pets) pet
- Chimpchompas - Requires 99 hunter, any MR greegree, and 1 banana per catch as bait
- [Sandy](/bso/custom-items/pets#miscellaneous-pets) - 3x hunter loot (2x sand gecko loot w/ Hunter Master Cape)
- [Hunter Master Cape](/bso/custom-items/capes/#master-capes) - 2x Speed boost to Hunter, +4 birdhouses, 2x loot from Sand Geckos with Sandy equipped, 2x implings
- [Quick Trap](/bso/skills/invention/#inventions) - 25% Speed boost to Hunter

### Optimal Gear Setup

Hunter master cape, Sandy

Wildy setup - Royal d'hide body and chaps (cost effective)
