## Custom Content and Boosts

- [**Blacksmith Equipment**](/bso/custom-items/skilling-outfits/#blacksmith-equipment) – +10% XP to [[/smith]] and [[/smelt]], works from bank
- [**Takon**](/bso/custom-items/pets#miscellaneous-pets) – 4× boost to [[/smith]] _(does not stack with Dwarven Greathammer)_
- [**Dwarven Ore**](/bso/monsters/bosses/king-goldemar#loot) – Used to smith dwarven bars
  - Requires Klik and [[smithing:99]] to smelt
  - 35% chance to fail when smelting (ore is consumed on failure)
- **Dwarven Bar** – Used to make various [Dwarven Equipment](/bso/custom-items/#dwarven-equipment)
  - Must equip full Blacksmith Outfit to smith bars
- [**Dwarven Greathammer**](/bso/custom-items/#dwarven-equipment) – 2× boost to [[/smith]] _(does not stack with Takon)_
- [**Dwarven Gauntlets**](/bso/custom-items/#dwarven-equipment) – 2× boost to [[/smelt]] _(does not work on gold bars at blast furnace)_
- [**Smithing Master Cape**](/bso/custom-items/capes/#master-capes) – Halves failure rate on smelting, 2× boost to Blast Furnace
- [**Scroll of Efficiency**](dungeoneering-training/dg-rewards#buyable-boosts-utility) – Saves 15% of bars used

## Optimal Gear Setup

- Takon
- Smithing Master Cape
- Blacksmith Outfit equipped in skilling
